Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, a tomato-based sauce, and a mixture of cheese, typically including ricotta and mozzarella. The layers are typically repeated several times, creating a hearty and savory casserole-like dish that is often baked in the oven until the cheese on top is melted and bubbly. Additional ingredients such as ground meat, vegetables, or herbs may also be added to the layers to enhance the flavor and texture of the dish.

Ingredients

------------------------------------------

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Splash of olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • 2 bay leaves

Lasanga:

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles (no boil preferred)
  • Splash of olive oil
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese to your liking
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a large saucepan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, basil, and bay leaves. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Add a layer of ricotta cheese on top of the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top of the ricotta cheese.
  10. Add another layer of sauce on top of the cheese.
  11. Repeat steps 7-10 until all ingredients are used up.
  12. Add a final layer of lasagna noodles on top.
  13. Add the remaining mozzarella cheese on top of the noodles.
  14. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  16. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
